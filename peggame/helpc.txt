Move (Move a peg directly): move r1 c1 r3 c3
From (Pick a peg): from r1 c1
To (Move the chosen peg - Must be used immediately after from command): to r3 c3
Cheatmode: cheatmode <on or off>
Undo (Undo last moves): undo <no. of moves>
Hint (Gives all possible moves): hint 
Quit (Quits the game): quit
Reset (Ends the current game and starts a new one): reset
